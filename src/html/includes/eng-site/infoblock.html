Important Information

We need to understand the exact diameters of future holes, as well as the possibilities of their approximate (or exact) wall thickness and material.

Be aware in advance in which room (or street) work will be done. If the room is already newly renovated, we can drill a hole without dust and dirt, but we must know this in advance, since in order to fulfill this tasks, an additional equipment is required.

For drilling we need:
  • - access to water(tap or hose from which we periodically can fill up our tank);
  • - access to electricity(voltage may vary from 3 to 5 kilowatt);
If the place of drilling works has limitied access, please provide us with preliminary photos(photo) and send them to us via viber (+380508047806).

Drilling at heights (over 3 meters) is carried out only with scaffolding. Therefore please warn us about this in advance.

We carry out any amount of work. (One hole in a residential building for a cooker hood may take 1-2 hours taking into account of the preparatory work and tool drift).
